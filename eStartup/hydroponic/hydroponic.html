Frequently Asked Questions

What is hydroponics?

Hydroponics is a method of growing plants without soil using nutrient-rich water.

How many plants can be grown at a time?

You can grow multiple plants depending on the size of the system.

What all can be grown?

Herbs, leafy greens, and even some fruits!

How many times do we need to change the nutrient water?

Typically every 1-2 weeks, depending on the plant growth stage.
