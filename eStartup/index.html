Frequently AskedQuestions

At LeafGrid, we know starting with home gardening and hydroponics can raise questions. Our compact spiral systems make indoor gardening easy and enjoyable. From how it works to what you can grow, we’re here to help you bring fresh produce and greenery into your home effortlessly!

1. What is hydroponics?

Hydroponics is a method of growing plants in a nutrient solution, rather than soil. This technique offers several advantages, such as reduced water consumption, precise nutrient control, and the ability to grow plants in confined spaces.

2. What types of plants can be grown in a hydroponic system?

You can grow a wide variety of plants, including herbs, vegetables, and even some flowering plants. Popular choices include lettuce, basil, tomatoes, and strawberries.

3. How much maintenance is required for a hydroponic system?

Maintenance typically involves checking the nutrient solution levels, cleaning the system, and monitoring plant health. Hydroponic systems reduce the amount of hands-on care required.

4. How often does the nutrient solution need to be replaced or replenished?

the nutrient solution should be checked and replenished every 1-2 weeks. However, it's important to monitor the solution regularly for signs of depletion or contamination, such as cloudiness or a strong odor.

5. Can anyone use hydroponics?

Absolutely! Our hydroponic system is designed to be user-friendly and hassle-free. With its simple setup and minimal maintenance, it's perfect for beginners and experienced gardeners alike.
